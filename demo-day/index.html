Demo Day - June 9th, 2025

Welcome to Demo Day - June 9th, 2025 for the Spring 2025 AR/VR Capstone!

Our students will present their AR/VR projects from this past quarter in the Allen Atrium at Allen Center.

Attendees are strongly encouraged to wear masks. Headsets (Quest 3) will be sanitized between every use.

Schedule

Time: Tuesday, June 9th -- 2:00 - 4:00 PM Pacific Time
Location: Held in the Allen Center for Computer Science & Engineering
Description: Come try seven new AR/VR + AI applications and meet the student developers! Each project has a space in the Allen Atrium of the Allen Center and all applications were developed by the students of the 2025 AR/VR Capstone class

Team Projects

Maze Runner

  • Asymetric multiplayer game where a VR runner must work together with an AR player from above the maze to capture the idol in the maze and escape from the robots.

ARC Car Racing

  • A co-located AR multiplayer RC car racing experience where players place checkpoints in their real-life environment to procedurally generate a racetrack.

Arcane Tiki Bar

  • You are a castaway, cursed for trespassing on sacred land. Your only hope for salvation? Craft a drink in your place using hand-track flourish and temperature haptics.

XRLibrary

  • XRLibrary is a mixed-reality library app that elevates the traditional reading experience through immersive environments, 3D contextual popups, and interactive annotations.

TidyBear

  • Make laundry folding fun with your TidyBear AI assistant! Earn wool for properly folded clothes to customize your bear.

Wizard Theft Auto

  • A mixed reality multiplayer wizard battle where players drawing symbols in AR to cast spells and use their environment to dodge attacks.
