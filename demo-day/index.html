Team Projects

Team 1: CSE2 Virtual Tour

  • CSE2 Virtual Tour is a VR application that allows the prospective students of the Allen School to conduct the tour in the Gates Center without being physically there. There is also a husky, Dubs, as the virtual guide to guide the user to complete a couple of interactive tasks during the tour.

Team 2: VR Dicom Visualizer

  • VR Dicom Visualizer is a VR project that aims to ease the communication between a patient and a doctor. It visualizes 3D medical images in VR to make it easier for a doctor to communicate to the patient about their health.

Team 3: Penguins Can Fly

  • You’re a penguin - you’ve always dreamed of soaring through the sky, but you’re a chubby penguin. Now’s your chance to fly! Maneuver through the air with your hang glider, dodge obstacles to achieve high scores, and play as a party game with your friends in local multiplayer mode (which only requires one Oculus!)

Team 4: Global Grocery

  • Global Grocery is a VR application that aims to explore the cost of living in different countries through grocery shopping. Users will make their way through three separate stores located in different countries and try to spend as close to $30 USD as possible. By comparing the amount of food that can be bought in different locations for the same amount of money, users can learn more about purchasing power in other countries.

Team 5: Bon Appétit

  • Team up with your friends and complete dinner orders against the clock! Featuring multiple kitchens, 5 unique dishes, and real-time feedback, Bon Appétit is a co-op VR line cooking experience that will surely put your teamwork and skills to the test. No cooking or restaurant experience needed!

Team 6: Sonic Bloom

  • A chill flight simulator for someone looking to get away from super realistic controls and environments or a very intense war-like flight game. We hope people enjoy "flying" and enjoy the environment in a completely stress-free experience.

Team 7: Extreme Red Light Green Light

  • "Extreme Red Light Green Light" is a more complex version of the game "Red Light Green Light," a game where players race to a finish line while trying to avoid moving when the "red light" is called, only being able to move while the "green light" is called. In addition to these features, this application consists of obstacles that require users to climb, row, and jump in order to advance. The target audience of this application is anyone who is interested in competing with others and being able to engage in physical activities at the same time.

Team 8: Boardless

  • Boardless is a VR application that provides STEM students with a virtual space to better communicate thoughts and share ideas with 3D models or dangling whiteboards. Users can easily import/export ideas using a variety of brushes and colors, 3D shapes, OCR technology, and other assets.
